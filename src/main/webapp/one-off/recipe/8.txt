Mini Buffalo Chicken Balls

http://images.therecipedepository.com/uploads/1758-mini-buffalo-chicken-balls.jpg

Ingredients:
2 tbsp vegetable oil
3 tbsp unsalted butter
1/2 cup hot sauce (we used Crystal)
1 pound ground chicken
1 large egg
1/2 celery stalk, minced
1 tbsp shallot, minced 
3/4 cup bread crumbs
1 tsp salt

Steps:
<div class="instruction">Preheat the oven to 450&deg;F. Drizzle the vegetable oil into a broiler-safe baking dish and use your hand to evenly coat the entire surface. Set aside.&nbsp;</div>
