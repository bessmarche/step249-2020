Butterscotch Pie

http://images.therecipedepository.com/uploads/1245-butterscotch-pie.JPG

Ingredients:
1 cup brown sugar
4 tbsp. cornstarch
1/2 tsp. salt
2 cups milk
2 egg yolks, beaten
1 tbsp. butter
1 tsp. vanilla
1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
optional: butterscotch chip

Steps:
Combine brown sugar, cornstarch, salt, and milk in pot on stovetop. Cook on medium heat and stir constantly until it thickens.<br />
Combine brown sugar, cornstarch, salt, and milk in pot on stovetop. Cook on medium heat and stir constantly until it thickens.
Mix in egg yolks; stir, and continue cooking until thickened.
Remove from heat. Add butter and vanilla, then stir again.
Pour into cooked pie crust. Bake at 400 degrees for 5 minutes or until brown.
