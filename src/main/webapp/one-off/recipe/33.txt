French Country Soup with Toulouse Sausages

http://images.therecipedepository.com/uploads/2437-french-country-soup-with-toulouse-sausages.jpg

Ingredients:
6 Toulouse sausages
1/2 cup lardons
2 tbsp butter
1 onion, chopped
4 -6 garlic cloves, chopped
1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
1 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
1 lb potatoes, coarsely cubed
4 cups chicken broth
Salt and pepper
2 large handfuls of chard, chopped
1 cup heavy cream

Steps:
In a non stick pan, brown the sausages, then slice in half vertically and cut into chunks. &nbsp;Set aside. &nbsp;Wipe out the pan with a paper towel, add the lardons and cook until it begans to brown. &nbsp;Add the butter, onion and garlic, then saute until the onions soften, then pour into a &nbsp;soup pot with the potatoes, broth, salt and pepper. &nbsp;Bring to a boil, then simmer for 20 minutes. &nbsp;Add the sausage chunks and continue to simmer for 10 minutes. &nbsp;Stir in the chard until wilted, then add the cream and cook until just hot.
