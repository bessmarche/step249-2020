Coco de Paimpol Beans

http://images.therecipedepository.com/uploads/2344-coco-de-paimpol-beans.jpg

Ingredients:
1/2 cup lardons or diced bacon
1/2 large onion, chopped
2 large garlic cloves,&nbsp;slivered
12 cherry tomatoes, quartered or 2 normal tomatoes, seeded and diced
1 tbsp fresh basil, chopped
1 tbsp fresh oregano, chopped
2-3 cups of shelled fresh coco beans
3 cups chicken broth
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper

Steps:
Brown the lardons, remove from pan and set aside. &nbsp;Add the onion and garlic to the fat in the skillet and cook until the onion is soft. &nbsp;Add the tomatoes and herbs, then cook for a minute or two. &nbsp;Add the beans, lardons, broth, salt and pepper, bring to a boil, then simmer for about 50-55 minutes.
