Slow Cooker Breakfast Casserole

http://images.therecipedepository.com/uploads/1537-slow-cooker-breakfast-casserole-1441650428.jpg

Ingredients:
1 (32 oz) bag frozen hash brown potatoes
1 pound bacon diced, cooked and drained
1 medium onion, diced
1 green bell pepper, diced
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese
1 dozen eggs
1 cup milk
1 tsp salt
1 tsp peppe

Steps:
Place a layer of potatoes on the bottom of the slow cooker, followed by a layer of bacon, onions, peppers and cheese.  Repeat the layering process two or three more times (until ingredients are used up), ending with a layer of cheese.<br />
