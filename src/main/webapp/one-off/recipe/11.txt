Thai-Style Chicken Salad Sandwiches

http://images.therecipedepository.com/uploads/1869-thai-style-chicken-salad-sandwiches.jpg

Ingredients:
2 bone-in, skin-on chicken breasts (ours were pretty large - if you are using smaller chicken breasts, you should use 3)
2/3 cup coconut milk 
1-1 1/2 tsp Thai green curry paste (depending on how much heat you want)
1-inch piece fresh ginger, peeled and cut into thin rounds
1 tbsp lime juice 
1 tsp fish sauce 
2 tsp serrano chili, sliced 
1 tbsp cilantro, 

Steps:
Rinse chicken and pat dry.&nbsp; Combine all ingredients except chicken.&nbsp; Pour mixture over chicken in a resealable plastic bag.&nbsp; Allow to marinate in fridge for at least 1 hour.&nbsp; Remove chicken from the refrigerator 15 minutes before cooking.&nbsp; Remove chicken from plastic bag and discard excess marinade.&nbsp; Pat chicken dry.&nbsp; Season with s&amp;p.<br /><br />Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.&nbsp; Heat a 12-inch cast-iron skillet (or other heavy, oven-safe skillet) over medium to medium-high heat.&nbsp; Add peanut oil.&nbsp; Once oil is hot, place chicken in the skillet skin-side down.&nbsp; Brown for 1-2 minutes.&nbsp; Turn the chicken over and place skillet in oven.&nbsp; Roast chicken for 30 minutes (until chicken juices run clear when you pierce the chicken with a sharp knife).&nbsp; Remove from oven and set aside to cool for 15-20 minutes.&nbsp; After the chicken has cooled, shred it with two forks.<br /><br />Combine lime juice, fish sauce, salt, chili powder, cilantro, mint, sugar, shallot, scallion and chili in a large nonreactive mixing bowl.&nbsp; Add shredded chicken and stir in the mayonnaise.&nbsp; Season to taste with additional salt, mayo or lime
