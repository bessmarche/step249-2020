Aztec Quiche

http://images.therecipedepository.com/uploads/1258-aztec-quiche-1441663731.jpg

Ingredients:
1 1/4 c. grated Monterey Jack Cheese
3/4 c. grated mild cheddar cheese
1 9-inch deep-dish frozen pie shell
1 4-oz. can diced green chilies
1 c. half and half
3 eggs, beaten lightly
1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. ground cumi

Steps:
Spread Monterey Jack Cheese and half of cheddar over bottom of pie shell.  Sprinkle diced chilies over cheeses.  Stir together in bowl the half-and-half, eggs, and seasonings. Pour carefully into pie shell.  Sprinkle with remaining cheddar. Bake uncovered in a preheated 325 degree oven for 50-55 minutes.
